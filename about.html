ABOUT US





Urban fashion is a renowned fashion clothing brand that was founded in 2010 . Under the vision of creating attractive and comfortable outfits for women of every age and persona, Urban fashion is working hard to provide its customers with high-quality products at a very affordable price range. A hardworking team of highly qualified professionals, design and textile creatives, marketing strategists, and finest crafters work together to present you with the most appealing and trending outfits ever. Be it stitched or unstitched, western or eastern, formal or semi-formal, men, women or girls, fragrances or body mists, accessories, bags or footwear, Urban fashion proudly excels at every niche. Our clothes are confected with vibrant color palettes, finest embroideries, delicate embellishments, smooth fabrics, and flawless prints. We fabricate designs with an aura of cultural heritage as well as true modern styles. By infusing classic styles with contemporary cuts, we create outfits representing sophistication and elegance. The collections include some bold, funky and playful ensembles as well. Our designers have the best eye to detail as they always keep in mind the demand of our customers and have been loyal to them all these years. We have at Urban fashion the top management with a well-organized workspace, effective policies, utmost discipline in operations, and excellent manufacturing standards. Our in-house production operations include the process of product designing, fabric selection, inspection, pattern making, crafting, and sewing, as well. We have more than 80 outlets at the busiest locations and malls in almost every city of Pakistan. Our stores are well equipped and fully stocked along with amazing aesthetics, a fine shopping environment, professional assisting staff, excellent quality service, and a convenient display of products. Choose the best. Choose Urban fashion!.
